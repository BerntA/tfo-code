You can have maximum 12 items. 
You can drop / use items as you wish.

Notice: In TFO v2.8 and lower you had to link every item to an ID in a manifest file, this is no longer necessary.

Commands:

tfo_inventory_add_item <scriptName> - Adds your item to the inventory through the console.
tfo_inventory_remove_item <scriptName> - Removes your item from the inventory thourgh the console, this will not drop the item but delete it entirely.
